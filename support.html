General queries

Your username

Your username is designed to identify you on Spotify, so you can't change it.
You don’t need your username to log in. Instead, use your email address and password or log in with the method you used to sign up e.g. Facebook, Apple, or your phone number.

Your display name

A display name replaces the username where it shows on your profile, app, and playlists. Note: You can’t use your display name to log in.

Account overview
